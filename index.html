About Me

A Ugandan born on 17th of October, 2005 named Kennedy Mwebaza a.k.a Ken Kayz Lee released his first official funny video in 2018 - the money after money. What started as a kid's journey into the digital world, driven by a desire to create and explore, will soon be developing into one of the most exciting producers Uganda will offer in the next years to come. Kennedy’s first release with the Ugandan Boys Entertainment marked the beginning of an awesome journey, release date November 25th. Growing up in the digital era, Kennedy found an early interest in technology which later turned into a fascination for programming and graphic design. In 2018 he started making videos on his smartphone, and with the help and feedback from his online friends his videos soon caught the attention of the numerous labels. ”Videos kind of just happened spontaneously a few years back. I found a video on Youtube I liked a lot – it inspired me and it made me start exploring producing myself. Everything started online with people, or rather nicknames, from around the world helping each other out. Now making videos are a big part of my life and what I want to pursue. I even started loving animation! My friend has always created animations, but it never really felt as my thing, but now I am enjoying it - creating what I feel like and exploring new things. Kennedy is working on upcoming videos while finishing his last year of high school in Wakiso, Uganda. He still has a big community of friends, fellow producers and supporters online - always commenting and giving him feedback on his work, which he describes as an important part of his progress, leaving him forever grateful.
